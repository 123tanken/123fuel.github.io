1-2-3 Fuel

Download the intuitive 1-2-3 Fuel app for free and start saving money now. The iPhone app is available in the App Store and the Android app in the Google Play Store for free.

Always Up-To-Date Fuel Prices

With the 1-2-3 Fuel App you can easily find the current fuel prices for Germany, France, Italy, Spain and Portugal. The fuel stations report the required data directly to a central system and we forward it to you immediately.

Map With All Fuel Stations

Whether you want to find fuel stations near you or in another city: In the map view you can find all important information at a glance.

Fuel Stations Along Your Route

You plan to drive a longer distance and want to refuel on the way as cheap as possible? No problem! Just enter your start and destination and 1-2-3 Fuel will show you the cheapest petrol stations on the way.

Neat List View

You want to see right now where it makes the most sense to refuel? In the list view you can immediately see which fuel stations are the cheapest. And to make sure you don't consume more fuel on your way to the gas station than you save in the end, all gas stations are grouped by distance.

All Your Favorites At A Glance

You have certain fuel stations where you prefer to fill up and want to keep an eye on them? With the 1-2-3 Fuel app you can create a favorites list. So you always have the prices of your favorite fuel stations available at a glance.

Always Up-To-Date

Thanks to the price alert you are always up-to-date even without opening the app. Simply select for which fuel stations and fuel types you would like to get notified about price reductions. You will then receive a notification directly on your smartphone as soon as the price falls.

Interactive Price History

For every fuel station you can view interactive price statistics. This makes it even easier to determine the ideal time to refuel.

Always Well Informed

With the 1-2-3 Fuel widget for the notification ceter you can always see the prices of your favorite fuel stations without having to open the app.

Apple Watch App

1-2-3 Fuel is also available for the Apple Watch. You can check the prices of your favorite fuel station or find the cheapest fuel station in the area fast and easy, directly on your wrist.
