1-2-3 Fuel

Download the intuitive 1-2-3 Fuel app for free and start saving money. The iPhone app is available in the App Store and the Android app in the Google Play Store.

Up-to-the-minute fuel prices

With the 1-2-3 Fuel App you can easily find the current fuel prices. The app shows the official fuel price data from the stations in real time.

Map view of all stations

No matter whether you want to find petrol stations near you or in another city: The map view gives you an immediate overview of all important information.

Fuel stations along your route

You plan to drive a longer distance and want to refuel as cheap as possible on the way? No problem! Simply enter your start and destination and 1-2-3 Fuel will show you the cheapest fuel stations on your way.

Smart list view

You want to see immediately where to best refuel your car? In the list view you can immediately see which fuel stations are the cheapest. And to make sure you don't waste more fuel on your way to the gas station than you save in the end, all gas stations are grouped by distance.

Your favorites, just one tap away

Do you have fuel stations where you prefer to fill up and want to keep an eye on them at all times? With 1-2-3 Fuel you can create a list of favorites. So you can always keep track of the prices at preferred fuel stations.

Always up to date

With the smart price alert you are always up to date even without opening the app. Simply set for which petrol stations and fuel types you would like to be informed about price reductions. You will then receive a notification directly on your smartphone as soon as the price drops.

Interactive price history

You can view interactive price statistics for each fuel station. This makes it even easier to determine the ideal time to refuel.

Always well informed

With the 1-2-3 Fuel widget for the iOS notification center, you can always see the prices at your favorite fuel stations without having to open the app.

Apple Watch app

1-2-3 Fuel is also available for Apple Watch. In the watch app you can check the prices of your favorite fuel station or find the cheapest stations at your current location, all directly at your wrist.
